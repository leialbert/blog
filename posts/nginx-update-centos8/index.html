CentOS 8.3 upgrade default nginx

December 26, 2020

Add the repo of University of Science and Technology of China

vim /etc/yum.repos.d/nginx.repo

Add the contents to the file：

[nginx-mainline]
name=nginx mainline repo
baseurl=http://mirrors.ustc.edu.cn/nginx/mainline/centos/$releasever/$basearch/
gpgcheck=0
enabled=1
module_hotfixes=true

Update the nginx

yum update nginx

Check the version of the nginx

nginx -v
